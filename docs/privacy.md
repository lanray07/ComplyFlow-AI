# Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 18, 2026

ComplyFlow AI helps businesses create and manage inspections, SOPs, incident reports, audit summaries, reminders, corrective action plans, photos, notes, and operational compliance reports.

This draft policy must be reviewed and customized for your legal entity, contact details, operating region, and production backend before public release.

## Information You Provide

The app may store business profile details, locations, team size, inspection records, incident reports, SOPs, audit records, reminders, safety notes, photos, and voice-to-text notes that you enter or upload.

## Local Storage

The current app stores operational records locally on your device using Apple platform storage. If cloud sync, account login, analytics, crash reporting, or a production AI backend is added, this policy and the App Store privacy labels must be updated before release.

## Photos, Camera, Microphone, And Speech Recognition

ComplyFlow AI requests camera and photo library access so you can attach inspection and incident evidence. The app requests microphone and speech recognition access so you can dictate field notes. The current app stores the resulting text note and does not intentionally keep persistent audio recordings.

## Purchases

Subscriptions are processed by Apple using StoreKit and In-App Purchase. ComplyFlow AI reads subscription entitlement status to unlock paid features.

## AI Features

The current build uses mock AI by default. A placeholder exists for a secure backend AI endpoint. Do not send business records, images, or notes to an AI backend until a production privacy, security, retention, and data-processing policy has been implemented.

## Disclaimers

ComplyFlow AI provides operational drafting and organization support only. It is not legal advice, regulatory certification, safety certification, or a replacement for licensed compliance, legal, safety, or trade professionals. AI suggestions must be reviewed before use. Users remain responsible for operational compliance.
