## Description

ComplyFlow AI helps small businesses and operational teams reduce paperwork, document inspections, prepare reports, manage recurring compliance tasks, and organize safety workflows from mobile devices.

Built for construction, roofing, landscaping, logistics, warehousing, cleaning companies, facilities management, and property management teams, ComplyFlow AI centralizes everyday operational compliance activity in one app.

Key features:

- Create site, vehicle, PPE, equipment, fire safety, cleaning, property, contractor, warehouse, and custom inspections
- Upload inspection and incident photos
- Capture voice-to-text field notes
- Generate AI-assisted inspection summaries, SOPs, audit summaries, and corrective action plans
- Track incidents, injury level, witness notes, and follow-up checklists
- Monitor audit readiness, high-risk gaps, overdue tasks, and reminders
- Export inspection reports, incident reports, SOP PDFs, audit summaries, and corrective action plans
- Store operational records locally with SwiftData
