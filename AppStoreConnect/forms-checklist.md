# App Store Connect Required Forms Checklist

Only the Apple Developer Program Account Holder or authorized Admin can complete many of these. Do not share your Apple ID password, 2FA code, taxpayer ID, or banking credentials with anyone.

## Apple Developer Program

- Enroll at `https://developer.apple.com/programs/enroll/`
- Choose Individual or Organization enrollment.
- Complete identity/business verification.
- Accept the Apple Developer Program License Agreement.
- Pay the annual membership fee.

## App Store Connect Access

- Sign in at `https://appstoreconnect.apple.com/`
- Confirm the account has access to Apps, Agreements, Tax, and Banking.
- Add additional users only if needed.

## Agreements, Tax, And Banking

- Accept required Apple agreements.
- Sign the Paid Apps Agreement before offering paid subscriptions.
- Add bank account information for proceeds.
- Complete required tax forms. Apple currently notes all developers must complete a U.S. tax form for the Paid Apps Agreement, with additional forms depending on account location.

## App Record

- Create new app.
- Platform: iOS
- Name: ComplyFlow AI
- Primary language: English (U.K.)
- Bundle ID: `com.complyflowai.app`
- SKU: `COMPLYFLOWAI-IOS-001`
- User access: Full Access unless you need limited teams.

## Pricing And Availability

- Choose app availability territories.
- Choose free app price for the base app if using subscriptions.
- Configure subscription products separately.

## App Privacy

- Add functional Privacy Policy URL.
- Complete privacy questionnaire based on the shipping build.
- Update answers if backend AI, analytics, crash reporting, account creation, or cloud sync are added.

## App Information

- Category: Business
- Secondary category: Productivity
- Content rights declaration
- Age rating questionnaire
- License agreement: Apple Standard EULA or custom Terms of Use URL

## App Version

- Upload screenshots for all required device sizes.
- Add description, subtitle, keywords, support URL, marketing URL, copyright, review contact, and review notes.
- Attach subscription products to the app version if required by App Store Connect for first review.
- Upload build from Xcode or Transporter.
- Submit app and subscriptions for review together.
